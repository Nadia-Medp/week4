~ Introduction ~

The Originals, a spin-off of The Vampire Diaries, centers on three of the Mikaelson siblings: Klaus, Elijah, and Rebekah. The Mikaelson family is also knowns as "The Originals" since they are the first vampires to exist. Klaus is the son of a witch and a werewolf, and his quest to unleash his hybrid nature was shown in The Vampire Diaries. The backdoor pilot, "The Originals", debuted on April 25, 2013, and revealed that werewolf Hayley, with whom Klaus had a druken-one night affair in season 4 of The Vampire Diaries, is pregnant with his child, the first ever tribrid. Elijah, Klaus, Rebekah, and Hayley are all introduced as main or recurring cast members in The Vampire Diaries is season 3 or 4, as opposed to the cast members of The Originals, who are introduced until in the backdoor pilot or later in the series.

The series begins with the Mikaelson siblings returning to New Orleans for the first time since 1919, where a pregnant Hayley has landed. She is under capture by the witches who use her to manipulate Klaus into helping them against Marcel's iron fist.

The Mikaelson siblings, who had originally built the French Quarter, were forced to abondon it while escaping from their vengeful father, Mikael. He wished to kill them all and end the vampire species, which he viewed as unnatural and evil. In their absence, Marcel, Klaus's former protege and adoptive son and Rebekah's former lover took control of the French Quarter as self-proclaimed king. Klaus decides that they must take down Marcel and reclaim the city that once belinged to them, while also protecting their city from a war brewing between vampires, werewolves, and witches.

~ First Season ~

The first season describes how Klaus' mystical child is going to be born and the threats that the Originals must work together to protect her from. It also explores the war between vampires, witches, and werewolves. The season ends with the birth of the baby girl named Hopy. She is given to Rebekah to hide and protect her after Klaus and Hayley pretend the baby died to safeguard her protection. Hayley is transformed into a hybrid through Hope's blood after being murdered when giving birth.

~ Second Season ~

In the second season, the Mikaelson siblings face new threats from their past. Their parents Mikael and Esther, return, and Dahlia, Esther's sister, poses a serious danger to Hope. Dahlia ais to abduct and train Hope as she did with Freya, Mikael and Esther's supposedly deceased firstborn daughter. Esther has secretely promised Freya to Dahlia, who took Hope when she realized she was still alive. Seeking vengeance, Freya joins forces with the Mikaelsons to destroy Dahlia.

~ Third Season ~

The third season begins with the return of Lucien Castle, the first vampire sired by Klaus. Lucien informs Klaus of a prophesy that predicts the Mikaelsons' demise by friend, foe and family. In an effort to battle the Mikaelsons, Lucien works with Tristan de Martel and his sister Aurora, Klaus' first love/the first sired vampire by Rebekah and Elijah respectively. This season Tristan murders Hayley's husband, the werewolf Jackson, and Lucien murders Camille. This season also features the first crossover with The Vamipre Diaraies, as Stefan Salvatore travels to New Orleans seeking help.

~ Fourth Season ~

The fourth season takes place five years after the third season. Marcel, now an upgraded Original Vampire due to Lucien's creation, rules the city with an iron fist once more, while Halyey cures Freya, Elijah, Renekah, and Kol. Klaus suffers at Marcel's hands, as the other Mikaelson siblings strive to save him. However, the arrival of an evil ancient mystical menace, called the Hollow, puts themm all in danger, particularly Hope, who is targeted due to her magical abilities and tribrid nature. Later in the season, it is revelaed that the Hollow created the first werewolves, which Hayley is descended from. Hope is now seven years old and is developing magical abilities, which Freya helps her control and use.

~ Fifth Season ~

The fifth and final season of the series takes place seven years after the fourth season. Hope, now a teenager, attends the Salvatore School for the Young and Gifted. The other Mikaelsons are living their lives separetely, trying to keep the Hollow at bay through their distance. However, when Klaus and an amnesiac Eljiah reunite, Hayley goes missing and Nazi vampires arise, the entire family returns to New Orleans to fight these new brewing threats. This season also serves as a soft backdoor pilot, with short apperances from the Salvatore School and future Legacies characters Alaric, Landon Kirby, and the Saltzman twins.
