History

The Originals is an American Supernatural drama televison series that premiered on The CW on October 3, 2013. It is a spin-off of The Vampire Diaries and the franchise's first television series expansion on the parent series. The series follows Niklaus (Klaus) Mikaelson vampire-werewolf hybrid, and his family as they get involved in the supernatural politics of New Orleans' French Quarter.
